Rich Dad And Poor Dad

Robert

Rich Dad Poor Dad by Robert Kiyosaki contrasts the financial philosophies of two father figures: a traditional, struggling "Poor Dad" and a wealthy, entrepreneurial "Rich Dad." It emphasizes financial literacy, acquiring assets over liabilities, and building wealth through entrepreneurship and investments, encouraging a mindset focused on financial independence.
